#C.R.C Membership Rule-book:

*) Any person willing to be a member of the club must pay the registration fee in full.

*) Attending all seminars and workshops arranged by the club is mandatory for all members.

*) All members of the club must take part in the competitions arranged by the club.

*) A member can participate in the competition either as a volunteer or a participant.

*) Tools provided by the club must be handled with care. If any component is damaged and/or rendered unusable, a predefined fine must be paid within a given time.

*) Well-presented opinions regarding any of club activities will be praised. However, a member cannot protest against decisions taken unanimously.

*) If an action of a member is considered harmful to the clubâ€™s reputation, the club can revoke said person membership.


#C.R.C Funding Strategy:

*) Members can buy/borrow necessary tools and components from the club. This privilege only extends to club members.

*) If any enthusiast can present a promising project plan, the club can fund them partially/fully in competitions. Not to mention the project must be trustworthy.

*) If any member wins any competition, 30% of prize money should be deposited into club fund. The certificates/crests/awards will be for the member(s) to take, the club will not hold claims over the certificates.

*) if we face shortage of money for any trustworthy project , we have to collect fund from our self.


#C.R.C Refunding Strategy:

*) In case of borrowing: Members can take components/tools on loan, on condition that the items must be returned as they were given after the specified time for which it was taken.

*) Members must notify the club of their progress with the items. If at any case a member fails to notify updates within two weeks, their funding/loan will be canceled.

*) If any member damages a component, he/she must compensate money equal to the price of said component. Any rules can be changed if club core committee wishes so.

*You must accept the rules and regulations given above to fulfill the membership form.
